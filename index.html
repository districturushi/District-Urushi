Care Instructions
Urushi is one of the hardest and resilient natural finishes known to mankind. With little care it can stay magnificent for many decades. Following are few care instructions and important info related to Urushi lacquered products.
  • Urushi takes around 1 yr or so to cure thoroughly (last 2%). However, it is safe to handle at the time of shipping and is 90-98% cured.
  • In case you experience any allergic reaction. It is recommended to seek medical treatment and avoid using pen until it is left to cure further.
  • Avoid soaking any lacquered pieces in water for prolonged periods or ultrasonic baths.
  • Avoid exposure to extreme temperatures.
  • If necessary, Gently wash with tap water and mild detergent. Wipe it dry with clean microfiber cloth.
  • Ultraviolet rays are damaging to Urushi and can cause damage to layers. Never leave your pen in direct sunlight for an extended time. It may cause discoloration or hazing.
  • It is expected for urushi to change color over time. Enjoy this transformation!
  • Store in cool and slightly humid place for long term storage. Do not use de-humidifier.

About District Urushi

My name is Diwakar. I am fountain pen user & collector located in Northern Virginia. I started practicing lacquer work as hobby and learned urushi techniques by customizing personal collection. I consider myself a novice practitioner who has barely scratched the surface of Urushi techniques. My work is inspired by traditional and modern techniques. I love to experiment with & mix different techniques together to come up with designs as I go.
